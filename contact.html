Do you provide the AR hardware?

We are a software platform provider. However, we have strategic partnerships with Microsoft, Apple, and Meta, and can facilitate bulk hardware procurement for enterprise deployments.

Is my CAD data secure on your cloud?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end AES-256 encryption. For highly sensitive defense or medical applications, we offer fully air-gapped on-premise server deployments.

How long does a typical deployment take?

Initial software provisioning takes less than 24 hours. Custom integrations with your existing ERP or PLM systems typically take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity.
